Kendrick Lamar, Ed Sheeran win big at music ceremony

August 2024 · 5 minute read

Pulitzer winning rap superstar Kendrick Lamar took home six awards on the night. They are 'Top Rap Album', 'Top Streaming Songs Artist', 'Top Rap Artist', Top Billboard 200 Album,Top Streaming Song (Audio)and 'Top Rap Male Artist'.

Pop star Ed Sheeran who was nominated 15 times with Kendrick Lamar and Bruno Mars, won five awards, 'Top Artist', 'Top 100 Hot Artist', 'Top Song Sales Artist', 'Top Radio Song' and 'Top Radio Songs Artist'.
