America’s Got Talent runner up, comedian Taylor Williamson may have a new woman in his life. After countless holidays (specifically Valentine’s Day) spent with judge Heidi Klum, it appears that the two have gone their separate ways. This is a bittersweet day for AGT fans everywhere, since the long running joke may be over for good.

Taylor Williamson Posts Heartfelt Message for Ginger Gonzaga

On February 28, AGT comedian Taylor Williamson caught the attention of fans when he shared a long post about She-Hulk star Ginger Gonzaga. Within the post, many were quick to notice Williamson complimenting and even calling Gonzaga “my love.”

The carousel posted features a photo of them at The Grammy Awards, Ant-Man and the Wasp: Quantumania premiere, and more, including a video of them getting cozy. Within the caption, Williamson praises Gonzaga for not only her personality, but her talent.

“I’m so lucky to stand next to this shooting star and watch her shine so bright. She is the silliest, most passionate soul and brings it out in her art in the most captivating way,” he wrote. “Swipe and enjoy a scene from her new show and you’ll see what I’m talking about. She’s got all the cute puppies too.”

This isn’t the first time Williamson has posted about Gonzaga. The first was published on November 7, 2022. The caption features the comedian once again praising the actress.

“She’s the funniest, smartest, most empathetic soul I know. She makes me smile and laugh a whole bunch and inspires me like no one else,” he wrote. “Hey Ginger, thanks for being all the things.”

Within both posts, there is at least one photo/video featuring them kissing. I’d say this is a full hard launch for the AGT comedian. Since nothing is confirmed, we’re not sure when Williamson and Gonzaga began seeing each other. The earliest social media interaction that can be found is from April 14, 2022, when Williamson replied to one of Gonzaga’s tweets.

His History with Heidi Klum

While appearing on America’s Got Talent season eight, Williamson began his fake romance with Klum. Throughout the weeks of the competition, the comedian continually made jokes for Klum and even flirted with her.

Fans loved their bidding romance, so the show ensured it stayed around, no matter how annoyed Klum got at Williamson. Fans of the series were so invested in their relationship that it became a behind-the-scenes bit.

Up until this past Valentine’s Day, Williamson had shared appreciation posts for Klum. Each post featured intimate photos/videos from his AGT season. To clarify for those who may be confused, Williamson and Klum never officially dated. Although many had hope for this “couple,” it was just a joke.
