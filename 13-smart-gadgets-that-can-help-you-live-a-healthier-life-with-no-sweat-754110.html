13 Smart Gadgets That Can Help You Live a Healthier Life With No Sweat

May 2024 · 4 minute read

We like to say to ourselves, “Okay, I have to start running in the morning, following a diet, taking care of my weight, drinking healthy liquids, and getting enough sleep.” But later we often face certain difficulties that stop us from putting our ideas into practice. Fortunately, there are many smart gadgets that can take care of our health for us.

Bright Side wants to go and buy all these cool devices that take care of your whole body, saving priceless time and energy!

13. KOR Nava smart bottle

This bottle differs from others since it can make water from all sources tastier. It also cleans the water from odors and has a built-in straw for easier drinking. It also has a sustainable patented coconut shell filter and a handle to help you carry it around easier.

12. Santo quick dry towel

This towel can be used during training or while climbing mountains when you sweat a lot. It absorbs all liquids, dries fast, and can be easily carried anywhere.

11. Kinsa thermometer

This thermometer allows you to track body temperature changes, identify a disease, and contact a doctor. With the help of the app, you can track the “average body temperature” in your area, city, or country. You can find this device here.

10. Tinké pulse sensor

Tinké is a sensor that allows you to track your blood oxygen saturation, and heart and respiratory rate. Simply place your finger on the device and all the data will appear in the app. You can find the gadget here.

9. Polar H10 heart rate sensor

Put the Polar H10 on and it’ll track your heart rate data, determining the heart rate zones. It’s also waterproof (in case you like to swim) and stores up to 200 hours of training data. You can buy this device here.

8. Picooc smart scale

Picooc is one of the smartest and most affordable scales. It can track your weight changes and even helps control your health. It determines your weight, body mass index, body fat percentage, muscle mass, and other biometric data. All this data gets stored in your smartphone. You can buy the device here.

7. Kolibree toothbrush

Kolibree watches whether you brush your teeth correctly or not. It analyzes the process each time and sends the data to your smartphone. Then it gives you recommendations on how often you should brush your teeth and what zones you have to pay more attention to. With the help of the app, you can take part in a game which is really useful for kids who don’t like to brush their teeth. You can find the brush here.

6. Fitbook journal

Fitbook is a professional log that explains to you how to lose weight, keep fit, and follow a healthy diet. You can order the log from here.

5. Steripen water purifier

This Swiss water purifier uses ultraviolet rays to kill 99.9% of all harmful bacteria and viruses in water. It can be used for up to 50 treatments per charge and 300 charge discharge cycles. This is equivalent to 15,000 liters of water. You can buy this gadget here.

4. BioScarf

BioScarf isn’t just a warm scarf, it’s a device that filters the air and reduces the risk of respiratory system issues. This gadget is really useful for people with allergies or for those who live in polluted areas. The scarf is made of materials that protect us from pollutants, allergens, and viruses. You can buy it here.

3. Atmotube air quality monitor

Atmotube is a portable device that analyzes the air around you and informs you about excess amounts of harmful gases, volatile organic compounds, temperature changes, and humidity. The gadget is really useful if you suffer from asthma or allergies. You can buy it here.

2. HAPILABS smart fork

This device can help you control and change your eating patterns. It can also track your arm movements and inform you to eat slower. This feature will help you to eat less and not gain weight. The device can be found here.

1. The smart sleeping mask

Xiaomi sleeping mask saves you from insomnia and improves your quality of sleep. Sensors track the brain impulses and the mask finds suitable music that helps you fall asleep. As soon as you fall asleep, music gets lower and stops. In the morning, you hear music again. It helps you wake up without any stress. The mask can be found here.

Would you want to buy any of these gadgets?
