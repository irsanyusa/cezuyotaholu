Sensational 'Australia's Got Talent' Contortionists The Rybka Twins Set to Audition for 'AGT'

June 2024 · 3 minute read

Reading Time: 2 minutes

America’s Got Talent is about to witness the awe-inspiring skills of Teagan and Sam Rybka, better known as the Rybka Twins. As they prepare to compete on season 18 of America’s Got Talent, fans and viewers are eagerly anticipating the dazzling displays they will bring to the stage.

Who Are The Rybka Twins?

The Rybka Twins, Teagan and Sam, are exceptionally talented dancers and acrobats hailing from Perth, Western Australia. Born on June 22, 1995, in Perth, Australia, these talented sisters embarked on their acrobatic journey at the tender age of three, under the guidance of their acrobat mother.

The twins gained early recognition when they appeared on the Australian television show Australia’s Got Talent in 2013. Their talent shone through, and they made it to the semi-finals of the competition, establishing a strong foundation for their future careers.

On platforms like TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube, the Rybka Twins have shared dance videos, providing glimpses into their daily six-hour training regime and sharing a look into their lives as professional performers. They have garnered millions of fans who eagerly await their displays of athleticism and creativity.

The Rybka Twins will continue their journey and take the stage again on season 18 of AGT. Fans can catch their highly anticipated audition on Tuesday, July 18, at 8/7c on NBC, as they showcase their extraordinary talent to the panel of judges, including Simon Cowell, Heidi Klum, Sofia Vergara, and Howie Mandel. Stay tuned to witness their awe-inspiring performances and see how far their incredible journey will take them on AGT.

They Collaborated With Sofie Dossi For a Prank

In a lighthearted prank video posted on their YouTube channel, the Rybka Twins collaborated with fellow AGT alum Sofie Dossi. The video also showcased the trio’s creativity, sense of humor, and exceptional teamwork.

The video showed the twins as they embarked on a 24-hour challenge to create an epic contortion routine with Sofie Dossi, who had participated before on AGT. With limited time, they then faced the pressure of finishing the routine, rehearsing, and nailing the performance. The video takes viewers on their journey, showcasing the dedication and determination of both the twins and Dossi in pulling off such a feat.

Although the twins didn’t actually audition as a group with Dossi, the video was quite entertaining. Since their prank video was published last year, we can assume that their training with Dossi, and last year’s audience attendance inspired them to actually audition.

The Rybka Twins’ ability to adapt and collaborate with other talented artists is glimpsed through the prank audition, even though it was not real. Including Dossi brought a new dimension to their routine and also highlighted their versatility as performers.
