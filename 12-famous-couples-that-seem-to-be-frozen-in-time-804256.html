12 Famous Couples That Seem to Be Frozen in Time

July 2024 · 2 minute read

A lot can change in a decade or more, but not for these celebrity couples. Pharrell Williams and his wife, Helen Lasichanh, seem to go against biology. Pharrell has spoken about his skincare routine and credits exfoliating and drinking water to his youthful appearance. There are more famous couples who also seem to be sipping from the fountain of youth.

Bright Side is in awe of these partners that don’t seem to age. Here is a list of celebrity couples that defy the aging process.

1. Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck

The days of “Bennifer” began in 2002. They broke up in 2004 but rekindled their connection in 2021, 20 years after they met.

2. Beyoncé and Jay-Z

This couple is notoriously private, and they’ve kept a low profile over the years. The couple got married in 2008 and have 3 children together.

3. Paul Rudd and Julie Yaeger

Paul Rudd married Julie in 2003. The couple has 2 children together.

4. Pharrell Williams and Helen Lasichanh

The artist got married in 2013 to model and fashion designer Helen Lasichanh. Together they have 4 children. They had a son in 2008 and triplets in 2017.

5. Mila Kunis and Ashton Kutcher

They met in 1998 while filming That ’70s Show, but they didn’t start dating until 2012. They got married in 2015 and have 2 children together.

6. John Legend and Chrissy Teigen

The singer and model met in 2006, got engaged in 2011, and got married in 2013. They have 2 children together.

7. Rob Lowe and Sheryl Berkoff

Rob Lowe is married to Sheryl Berkoff, who is a makeup artist. They got married in 1991 and have 2 sons.

8. Jennifer Connelly and Paul Bettany

These 2 actors met while working on the film, A Beautiful Mind, and got hitched in 2003. The couple has a son and a daughter.

9. Angela Bassett and Courtney B. Vance

These actors got married in 1997 and have twins that were born via a surrogate.

10. Kristen Bell and Dax Shepard

Dax and Kristen began dating in 2007 and got engaged in 2010. They got married in 2013 and have 2 daughters.

12. Will Smith and Jada Pinkett Smith

The couple met in 1994 on the set of The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air and got married in 1997.
