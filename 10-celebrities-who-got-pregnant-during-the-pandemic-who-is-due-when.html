Is it just us, or have a lot of celebrities gotten pregnant during the pandemic? Now, the stars who first announced their buns in the oven several months ago are all either giving birth or expecting to give birth very soon.

Check out ten examples below, including a few talent show judges and contestants. If they haven’t given birth yet, it’s only a matter of time before we meet their little bundles of joy.

10 Celebrities Pregnant During The Pandemic

Emma Roberts

Emma Roberts announced last summer that she was pregnant with her first child with partner Garrett Hedlund. The actress reportedly gave birth before the new year, to a baby boy named Rhodes.

Emma had previously worried she wouldn’t be able to get pregnant due to her endometriosis. She even froze her eggs. “It sounds cheesy, but the moment that I stopped thinking about it, we got pregnant,” she said.

Witney Carson

The pro dancer on Dancing with the Stars just gave birth to her first child with husband Carson McAllister. Witney documented her birth process on Instagram, and shared that she ended up having a C-section after 24 hours in labor. She had a baby boy.

‘DANCING WITH THE STARS’ PRO WITNEY CARSON GIVES BIRTH TO FIRST CHILD

April Love Geary

The model, who is engaged to Masked Singer judge Robin Thicke, gave birth to their third child together in December. They had a baby boy, naming him Luca Patrick. The couple already have two daughters together, Mia and Lola.

April announced she was pregnant on Instagram in October of last year, posting a shot of herself on the beach, writing, “Sorry we can’t hang out, there’s a pandemic & I’m pregnant… again.” She added, “We love consistency!”

Meghan Trainor

The Voice UK coach is pregnant with her first child with husband Daryl Sabara. She recently posted a photo of her bare baby bump on Instagram, saying she had six weeks to go in her pregnancy. Meghan shared in October that she was expecting a baby boy.

MEGHAN TRAINOR IS ALL ABOUT THAT PREGNANT BELLY IN HILARIOUS NEW VIDEOS

Kelly Rowland

The Voice Australia coach announced in October that she was five months pregnant with her second child with her husband Tim Weatherspoon. The couple already has a 6-year-old son named Titan.

“We had been talking about it loosely, and then COVID happened, and we were just like, ‘Let’s see what happens,’” Kelly said when she announced the news, adding that it happened “right away.”

Ashley Tisdale

The new Masked Dancer judge is currently expecting her first child with husband Christopher French. Ashley announced her pregnancy in September, and later shared that she was expecting a baby girl.

ASHLEY TISDALE FLASHES BABY BUMP ON ‘THE MASKED DANCER’ — ALL ABOUT HER PREGNANCY

Gabby Barrett

The 20-year-old American Idol album and her husband Cade Foehner are expecting their first child together. The couple announced the pregnancy in August of last year, and revealed that they’re having a daughter, due early this year.

“We were expecting it to take a little while, and it seemed like the first try that it happened, so we were just so excited,” Cade said. “We were blown away that it worked so quickly. But yes, we were starting to plan on it, for sure.”

Mandy Moore

The This Is Us actress and singer is expecting her first child with husband Taylor Goldsmith. They announced the news in September, sharing that they would be welcoming a son in early 2021. On December 9, Mandy posted on Instagram that she was at 30 weeks.

Bindi Irwin

Bindi, who is the daughter of the late Steve Irwin, is currently expecting her first child with husband Chandler Powell. Last month, she shared that she was 26 weeks pregnant, and that her daughter was the size of a shingleback lizard.

Hilary Duff

The actress announced in October that she’s expecting her second child with husband Matthew Koma. The pair already has a daughter named Banks, and Hilary has a son named Luca from her previous marriage to Mike Comrie.
