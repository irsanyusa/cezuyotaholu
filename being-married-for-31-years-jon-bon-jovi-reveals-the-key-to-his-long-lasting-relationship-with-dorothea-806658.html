The Story of Jon Bon Jovi and Dorothea Shows That With the Right Person, a Relationship Will Last a

August 2024 · 3 minute read

People who are meant to be together will find each other no matter what. Jon Bon Jovi and Dorothea are proof that soulmates do exist and that 31 years of love can’t beat the vow, “In sickness and in health, to love and to cherish, till death do us part.”

There are so many interesting stories out there, and we at Bright Side think that this couple’s story is truly fascinating, so we conducted an investigation so you could learn more about this couple’s unique experience.

Jon Bon Jovi met Dorothea for the first time in 1980.

Jon Bon Jovi and his wife, Dorothea Bongiovi, give a good name to love. They show that marriages can last. After 31 years of marriage and having 4 children, the 58-year-old rocker is telling the world about his long marriage and love affair with his wife, as well as their secret.

After 31 years of marriage, the happy couple reveals that “mutual respect” is their secret.

Jon Bon Jovi explained that having mutual feelings for each other made it easier to grow up together. Adding to this, he stated, “We like each other. We want to hang out.” Then Dorothea added, “I always said I was good at spotting potential too. I have a gift.”

His relationship with his family always comes before his music career.

Their not-so-rock-and-roll marriage and close family life with their 4 kids, daughter Stephanie, 27; and sons Jesse, 25; Jacob, 18; and Romeo, 16; have kept them closer than ever.

Dorothea has been the singer’s rock throughout his career. He said, “We work hard at it, but we enjoy each other and we never fell for the trappings of what celebrity can do.”

He also stated that in their marriage, he was the crazy visionary and Dorothea was the one with the glue, needle, and thread, keeping it all together. However, he revealed that witnessing that would happen over the years to people that were close to them and people that they knew from afar.

Being friends at first was the key to their relationship.

The rock star said, “She let me cheat off her in history! From the moment I saw her, I was instantly attracted to her, and that hasn’t changed in the 40 years since I first saw her.” He also revealed that Dorothea was the inspiration for all of his sentimental ballads.

Even though their marriage was going well, the couple knew that they had to work through some rough times. It was better for them to work together to get through those bad times than to do it alone.

It’s a partnership.

The couple, who was determined to give their children a normal upbringing, also works together for the Soul Foundation, which they launched in 2006. The non-profit organization aims to tackle the issues of hunger and homelessness in the United States.

“It’s a partnership,” Dorothea said. Adding to her kind words, “You have to help people when you see them suffering or injustice happening, and having the ability to do that is quite a blessing!”

These best friends and lovers merged into one family.

“We make it work, everything is possible,” said Dorothea, also sharing that the 2 have had their hiccups along the way, but their dedication to each other has kept their bond strong. They’re clearly proud and excited about their happy life.
