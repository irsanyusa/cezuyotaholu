Hocus Pocus Is Back 29 Years Later and This Is What Its Actors Look Like Today

June 2024 · 1 minute read

Many people’s favorite children’s fantasy and comedy film, Hocus Pocus, was released almost 30 years ago, on July 16, 1993. We still remember the peculiar smiles of the Sanderson sisters, or the adorable romance between Allison and Max. And to know what’s going on in the lives of these characters after all this time, keep reading.

We at Bright Side set out to learn more about these actors and want to show you what they look like today.
