16 Celebrities That Shine on the Red Carpet but Dress Like Regular People in Their Everyday Lives

May 2024 · 1 minute read

Very often, we see famous people on the red carpet complete with perfect hair, stunning makeup, and amazing outfits by big designers. But it doesn’t mean that they look like this in their everyday lives. For example, Charlize Theron, Reese Witherspoon, and even Leonardo DiCaprio don’t worry about style and fashion rules a lot of the time. They don’t worry about other people’s opinions and look the way they want.

We at Bright Side watch famous people not only at official events. And we’re sure that if you suddenly feel that your everyday look isn’t good enough, you should remember that somewhere out there, Keira Knightley, Anya Taylor-Joy, or some other famous person is walking around, not worrying about their style for a single second.
